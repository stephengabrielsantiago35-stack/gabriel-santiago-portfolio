Everything you need to know about working with me

I'm Gabriel Santiago, a multidisciplinary creative based in Dubai, UAE. I specialize in photography, videography, graphic design, and web development — offering end-to-end visual solutions for brands, events, and individuals.

I'm based in Dubai, UAE, and I'm available for projects throughout the UAE — including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, and all other emirates. Travel within the UAE is included in my project pricing.

I typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent requests, feel free to call or WhatsApp me at +971 50 397 2584.

I cover a wide range including:

  • Portrait Photography — Individual, family, and professional headshots
  • Event Photography — Corporate events, galas, and private celebrations
  • Product Photography — E-commerce, luxury goods, and catalog shoots
  • Lifestyle Photography — Brand storytelling and editorial work

My primary setup includes a Canon R5 mirrorless camera with professional RF lenses, Profoto lighting equipment for studio work, and DJI drones for aerial photography. I bring backup gear to every shoot.

Turnaround time depends on the project:

  • Portrait sessions: 5-7 business days
  • Events: 7-10 business days
  • Product shoots: 5-10 business days
  • Rush delivery: Available for an additional fee (2-3 business days)

I typically deliver professionally edited, high-resolution JPEG files. RAW files are available upon request for an additional fee, as they represent unfinished work. I believe the editing process is an essential part of the final product.

I provide full video production including:

  • Promotional & brand videos
  • Event highlight reels
  • Social media content (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
  • Training & educational videos
  • Drone videography

Every video package includes pre-production planning, on-location filming (with professional lighting and audio), full editing with color grading, background music licensing, and 2 rounds of revisions. Final delivery is in your preferred format (MP4, MOV, etc.).

Timeline varies by project complexity:

  • Short social clips (30-60 sec): 3-5 business days
  • Promotional videos (1-3 min): 7-14 business days
  • Event highlight reels: 5-10 business days
  • Full production projects: Discussed case-by-case

I offer a full range of graphic design and web development:

  • Logo & brand identity design

  • Marketing materials (brochures, flyers, social media graphics)
